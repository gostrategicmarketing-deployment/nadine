Family Financial Positioning

The biggest mistake parents make: Assuming FAFSA gives money.

FAFSA doesn't give money‚Äîit evaluates your tax return.

It looks at taxable income to calculate your Student Aid Index (SAI). This drives what colleges think you can "afford."

Many families' tax returns unintentionally position them to look wealthier than they are. With professional guidance, there are creative, ethical ways to position your finances to maximize both aid eligibility AND merit scholarship potential.
